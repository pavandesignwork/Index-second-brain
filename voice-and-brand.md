## How to fill this in

Your voice is what stops Claude's writing from sounding like every other AI output. Spend a few minutes here.

The fastest approach: find 2–3 things your company has actually written that sound right — a good Slack message, an email, a product update. Read them and notice:

1. **Sentence length.** Short and punchy, or long and flowing?
2. **Words you'd never say.** List the ones that feel wrong for your brand.
3. **How you open.** Straight to the point, or set up context first?
4. **How you close.** A next step? A question? Nothing extra?

Write those patterns down in plain language. You don't need to be a copywriter — just be honest about how your team actually communicates.
